Our Story

A hybrid model combining textile trade and digital capabilities

Textile Exports + Integrated Digital Services

Innovera Trading & Services is a hybrid trading and services company focused on textile exports and integrated digital solutions. The business follows a platform-based model similar to e-commerce marketplaces, sourcing finished products from manufacturing hubs like Surat and fulfilling global orders.

Alongside trading, Innovera provides in-house digital marketing support for branding, customer acquisition, and growth. The long-term goal is to move from a trading-led business to a fully integrated manufacturing and export ecosystem based in Jaipur.

Ready-made textile and apparel sourcing
Active export focus on USA and UK markets
In-house SEO, ads, and social media support
Future manufacturing setup planned in Jaipur

Challenges, Solution & Offerings

A focused response to quality, branding, and export execution gaps

Marketplace Quality Gaps

General marketplace models often lack strong quality control over third-party sellers, creating inconsistency for buyers.

Vendor Product Mismatch

Vendor-based sourcing can lead to product mismatch, making trust and repeat business harder to build.

Weak Brand Ownership

Trading-only or outsourced models often reduce brand control, lower alignment, and limit customization.

Manufacturing in Jaipur

Set up an in-house manufacturing unit in Jaipur, especially Sanganer, to improve control over production and consistency.

Own Catalog Design

Build a proprietary catalog instead of relying on third-party listings, improving presentation, positioning, and control.

Branding & Packaging

Introduce customized packaging, inserts, and remarketing tools to create stronger brand recall and customer assurance.

Integrated Supply Chain

Create an end-to-end export model spanning manufacturing, branding, marketing, and delivery.

Customer Assurance

Deliver exactly what is promised with a trust-based operational approach centered on reliability.

Global Positioning

Stay focused on international markets that offer stronger margins and more scalable growth opportunities.

Textile & Apparel Exports

Ready-made textile and apparel products sourced from Surat and delivered globally through a catalog-based platform.

Digital Marketing

SEO, social media campaigns, and paid ads that support better acquisition, visibility, and business growth.

Web Development & Branding
